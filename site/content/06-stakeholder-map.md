## Engagement Sequence Logic

Tier 1 contacts are **pull contacts** — they can unlock multiple Tier 2 and Tier 3 relationships with warm introductions. Never cold-email a Tier 2 contact if a Tier 1 partner can introduce you.

Tier 3 (funders, venues, media) operate independently and can be pursued in parallel to Tier 1/2.
