## 1. CGI Seattle — Meeting Request

**To:** `hoc.seattle@mea.gov.in`
**Subject:** Request for Meeting — India–USA Performing Arts Festival Initiative

> Dear Consul General Gupta and Team,
>
> I hope this message finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out on behalf of [Your Organization / Initiative Name] to request a meeting with your office regarding an India–USA cultural exchange initiative we are launching.
>
> We are developing an **inaugural bi-coastal India–USA Performing Arts Festival** focused on classical music, classical dance, and theater, anchored in Seattle and the San Francisco Bay Area. The initiative draws direct inspiration from the Consulate's successful India Culture Week at Seattle University in November 2024 and aims to scale that format into a multi-city pilot this year, building toward a larger annual festival in Year 2.
>
> We believe the Consulate General of India in Seattle would be the natural lead cultural partner given the Consulate's leadership on India Culture Week, your jurisdiction across the Pacific Northwest, and your track record of engaging state government, universities, and civic institutions around Indian cultural programming.
>
> Specifically, we would welcome your guidance on:
>
> 1. Official endorsement and co-presentation of the festival
> 2. Introduction and support for ICCR Travel Grant applications for participating Indian artists
> 3. Invitation-letter assistance for P-3 artist visa petitions
> 4. Introductions to relevant institutional partners in the Consulate's network
>
> We would be grateful for the opportunity to meet at your offices in Seattle, or via video call, at a time that suits your schedule. I have attached a brief 1-page concept note for your review in advance.
>
> Thank you for the Consulate's continued leadership in strengthening India–US cultural ties. We look forward to the possibility of collaborating.
>
> With warm regards,
> [Your Full Name]
> [Title / Organization]
> [Phone Number]
> [Email Address]
> [Website or LinkedIn, if applicable]

## Usage Notes

- **Customize, don't just send.** Replace all bracketed placeholders. Add a specific shared reference where you can (e.g., "I attended the Seattle University India Culture Week and was inspired by...").
- **Attach the concept note.** All external outreach emails should include a 1-page concept note. Draft this separately.
- **Follow up in 7 days.** If no response, a brief polite follow-up on Day 7 is appropriate. If no response by Day 14, try a different channel (phone call, LinkedIn, warm introduction).
- **Track responses.** Keep a simple spreadsheet of every outreach with date sent, date responded, and next action.
