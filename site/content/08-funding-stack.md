## Target Budget

| Scenario | Total Budget |
|---|---|
| Lean pilot | $175,000 |
| Mid pilot | $225,000 |
| Ambitious pilot | $275,000 |

The plan below targets the mid pilot ($225K) as the working number. The Seattle-led shape with one Bay Area flagship evening keeps the scope tighter than a full bi-coastal multi-day festival, which lets the lean and mid scenarios land closer to each other.

**Budget reduction factor:** A Seattle-based team running a Seattle-anchored festival saves roughly $15K–$30K in travel, lodging, and remote-coordination costs compared to a team running a true dual-city operation. Those savings partly cover the Bay Area flagship.

## Why This Stack

1. **No single funder is load-bearing.** If PAS doesn't come through in time, we still ship.
2. **Two corporate sponsors + one Indian focal sponsor carry the core budget.** These are relationship-based and faster than competitive grants.
3. **Public arts grants are "stretch upside."** If they land, we upgrade to ambitious pilot. If not, we do the mid pilot cleanly.
4. **Ticket revenue is real but modest.** Pricing should support access (mix of $15–$75 tickets across the festival).
5. **In-kind contributions are meaningful.** Venue rental alone at a major performing arts center can run $10K–$30K per night — if a university venue waives it as in-kind, that's a huge budget win.

## Grant-by-Grant Deep Dive

### ICCR Travel Grant
- **What it funds:** Round-trip economy airfare for Indian citizens ONLY
- **Not covered:** Hotels, visa fees, honoraria, board
- **Application route:** CGI Seattle or SF endorses, then ICCR approves
- **Timeline:** 8–12 weeks before travel; apply by Week 4–6 of our timeline for travel in Month 5
- **Success factors:**
  - Strong, personal invitation letter from US host
  - Indian Mission's endorsement (they are consulted)
  - Artist's professional profile (performances, reviews, awards in India)
  - Clear statement of how the trip "upholds Indian interests abroad"
- **Risk:** Airfare only — does not solve hotel, meals, honoraria. Budget for all of that separately.

### US Embassy New Delhi — PAS Small Grants
- **What it funds:** US-India cultural programming, arts workshops, exhibitions
- **Key requirement:** Must include an American cultural element or connection with American experts/institutions
- **Realistic in 6 months?** Risky. Review cycles historically 3–6 months. Check grants.gov for current open RFP.
- **Our angle:** Pair Indian masters with US-based Indian-origin artists and/or US academic experts. The co-presentation with a US university (Seattle U, UC Berkeley, etc.) directly satisfies the "American institutional connection" requirement.
- **Application path:** Submit via grants.gov under the specific opportunity number when open.

### California Arts Council
- **What it funds:** Project and program grants for California cultural organizations
- **Requirement:** Must be 501(c)(3) or fiscally sponsored
- **Open calls:** Check arts.ca.gov regularly; deadlines vary by program
- **Relevance:** San Francisco Bay Area anchor city makes this directly applicable

### 4Culture (King County)
- **What it funds:** Project grants for King County-based arts work
- **Cycles:** Rolling + quarterly depending on program
- **Relevance:** Seattle-side anchor; 4Culture has funded Seattle Center Festál Indian programming (Diwali) previously

### Washington State Arts Commission (ArtsWA)
- **What it funds:** Organizational and project grants
- **Relevance:** Seattle side; some programs require 501(c)(3) or equivalent

### Alliance for California Traditional Arts (ACTA) — Living Cultures Grant
- **What it funds:** Traditional arts programming in California
- **Precedent:** The Music Circle (LA) has received multiple Living Cultures grants for Indian classical programming
- **Fit:** Direct bullseye for traditional Indian performing arts pilot

### NEA Grants for Arts Projects
- **Realistic in 6 months?** **No.** NEA cycles are annual with 6+ month review. Plan for Year 2.
- **Worth knowing:** Arts Projects, Challenge America, and ArtWorks categories could all fit
